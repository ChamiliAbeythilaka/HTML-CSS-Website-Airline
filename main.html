CNS AIRLINE

CNS airline is the major airline in Sri Lanka and is a subsidiary of the CNS group. It is also the national airline of the country. Its major operation is from the Sri Lanka International Airport. It was founded on November 30th, 1995. The Hub of the CNS airlines operates services to fifty-five (55) destinations in about 45 countries, covering around 6 continents.
