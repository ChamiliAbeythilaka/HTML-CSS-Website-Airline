The first class is a luxury seating area of an aircraft that offers more space, comfort and service than other seats, ranging from private suites to board-board shower access.

Business class is a type of airplane seating that is more expensive than economy but less so than first class.Business class is usually reserved for business travelers. The service, seating, and food are usually the differences between first and economy.

Economy class is where complexity meets simplicity, creating the ideal combination for a pleasurable flight. Adjustable seats, delectable cuisine with a choice of up to three main courses (on long-haul flights), light snacks, teas, coffee, and complimentary bar service with a selection of wines, spirits, and soft drinks, and inflight entertainment with over movies for all ages - these are the little luxuries that make for pleasant travel memories for everyone on-board.
